About Me

Hi there! I’m someone who tends to stay quiet, not because I don’t have anything to say, but because I’m still learning how to say it. I’m introverted, soft-spoken, and usually the person sitting silently, observing more than speaking. Art is where I speak the loudest. It’s where I feel most like myself. I draw when I don’t know how to explain what’s going on in my head. It’s not always perfect, but it’s honest.

I don’t have some big story to tell. I’m just a quiet person, trying to get through life and understand myself a little better. Most days, I don’t really know what I’m doing... but when I draw, it feels like something makes sense. Like all the quiet in me finally has somewhere to go.

“The monotony and solitude of a quiet life stimulates the creative mind,” – Albert Einstein
